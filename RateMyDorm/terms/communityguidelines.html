Community Guidelines:

  • Be honest with your reviews. Users should be able to trust dorm ratings, opinions, and other users' feedback in general. Please do your part to make RateMyDorm a trustworthy site.
  • When commenting on a dorm, it is helpful to offer both pros and cons to the dorm. Please don't leave out any details you feel should be mentioned.
  • Please keep comments relative to the topic at hand.
  • Please do not contradite the opinions of others. RateMyDorm is not a place for debate. You may share your own opinions, but do not reference other comments. Move your conversation to Discord or contact Michael Komnick if you would like to disagree with an opinion.
  • Some of our Users have specific needs in a dorm. Please keep this in mind as you comment or review positively or negatively in a dorm category. What (dis)pleases you may not affect another!
  • Don't be annoying or toxic in the comments section, and don't feed the trolls. We know you like to troll too, but don't you dare do it here. Stay mad. Your mom. Cry about it.
  • No offensive language. Don't speak personally about other people. Talk down about the dorms if you want.
  • Don't spam in the comments or rate a dorm category multiple times with different accounts. Be reasonable.
  • Don't post any dangerous links in our comments sections. No bad intentions towards our other Users please.
  • Respect Sewanee's honor code, obviously!
  • Let us know if you catch someone being sus or breaking the rules (or Community Guidelines). You will get cookies (for your use on this website)!!
  • Follow these guidelines carefully, please. Don't become our enemy, because we can remove your comments and take away priveleges. We might even ban your account.
